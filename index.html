About me

I am a final year student taking MSc. Data Science at the African Center of Excellence in Data Science in the University of Rwanda. Currently, am a research intern at the Harvard University and working on my Machine Learning research project advised by Dr. Weiwei Pan, Harvard University and Dr. Melanie Fernandez Pradier, Microsoft Research.

I have an immense interest in Computer Vision; deep learning models in image recognition. My research is focused on using Transfer Learning in bean plant leaf disease classification using images of bean plant leaves in precision agriculture. My goal is to build a robust model that can accurately distinguish between various bean plant leaf diseases even in case of light alteration or angle rotation, towards improving the quality and quantity of agricultural products in East Africa.
