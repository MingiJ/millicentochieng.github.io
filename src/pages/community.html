Microsoft Africa Research Institute (MARI)

I am the organizer of MARI monthly seminars where we invite researchers to talk about their interesting works in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Human Computer Interaction (HCI) in/outside Africa. You can view our past Seminar events here and sign up to receive calendar invites for future Seminars.

Women in Data Science (WiDS)

As a WiDS Kenya/Africa Ambassador, I have been organizing and hosting WiDS Kenya and Africa Annual Conferences and WiDS Datathon workshops in Kenya since 2021. These events aim at inspiring and supporting women in Data Science, and educating everyone interested in Data Science regardless of their gender. Since 2022, I have served as a WiDS Ambassador Advisory Council. I am passionate about youth empowerment, especially mentoring girls and my goal presently is to build a healthy Data Science community where women can network and support one another as they keep thriving in the field of Data Science. All Data Science enthusiasts (regardless of gender) interested in joining Women in Data Science (WiDS) Kenya Community can reach out to me directly!
